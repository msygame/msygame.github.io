Delete Your Data and Account

At MsyGame, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal information. As part of our ongoing commitment to transparency and compliance with data protection laws, we provide the following options for managing your data and account with us.

Deleting Your Account

What Does This Mean?

Deleting your account means you want to permanently disable your account and remove your personal data from our records. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to access it again.

How To Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account, please send us an email at maszeyuen1@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account." Include your account username in the body of the email.

We will send you an email to confirm your request. After we receive your confirmation, we will proceed to delete your account. Please note that it may take up to 30 days to fully delete your account from our systems.

Considerations Before Deletion

Before you decide to delete your data or account, please remember:

You will lose access to your account and the game.

You will lose any in-game progress and purchases.

The deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Questions?

If you have any questions about deleting your data or account, please reach out to us at maszeyuen1@gmail.com. We're here to help!
